Frequently Asked Questions

How soon can I start lessons?

You can start within 24-48 hours of registration! We'll match you with a perfect teacher based on your goals and schedule your first lesson. Most students have their first lesson within 24 hours during business days.

Do you offer trial lessons?

Yes! Book a free 30-minute trial lesson to experience YoLingo's unique teaching style before committing. No credit card required for the trial. You'll get a real lesson with a native teacher and personalized feedback.

Can I switch teachers?

Absolutely! If the teaching style doesn't match your learning preferences, we'll find you a better match within 24 hours at no extra cost. We want you to have the best learning experience possible.

What if I need to reschedule?

We offer flexible rescheduling with 24-hour notice. For private lessons, we guarantee make-up sessions. Group classes provide recorded sessions and the option to join another session that week if available.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are secure and encrypted. We also offer installment plans for longer commitments.

Are your teachers certified?

Yes! All YoLingo teachers are native speakers with teaching certifications (DELF/DALF for French, DELE for Spanish) and university degrees. They have 3+ years of teaching experience and undergo regular training.
